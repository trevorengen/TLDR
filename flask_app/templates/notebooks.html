Notebook Usage

Use the dashboard to create bulletpoints of large pieces of text and then save them to a notebook, or you can simply add the bulletpoints yourself. Once you've loaded up your notebook with all the information you need, you can simply query your notebook with the textarea below and the computer will do its best to answer your question based off the information you've provided! Take notes, add them to a notebook, and then simply ask a question and the notebook will take care of the rest as well as provide a percentage of confidence in its answer!

The more information you provide to the computer the better your answers will be!
